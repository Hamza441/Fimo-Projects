Foundation for Immigrant and Minority Opportunities (FIMO)

Vision: FIMO envisions a future where every child, regardless of their background, has the opportunity
to excel in their education and in life, becoming active, engaged members of their community.
We aim to provide comprehensive support from kindergarten through college,
including homework assistance, enriching activities, inspiring field trips, invigorating
hiking experiences, and unwavering youth support. Our commitment extends to empowering
immigrant and minority communities by facilitating their path to U.S. citizenship, aiding in
job applications, simplifying the tax return process, and coaching and mentoring on college life
and success.
